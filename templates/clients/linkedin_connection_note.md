# Template: LinkedIn Connection Request Note
# Style: warm_networking
# Variables: contact_name, company_name, specific_context, reason_for_fit

---
SYSTEM INSTRUCTION FOR CLAUDE:
Write a LinkedIn connection request note. This is NOT a pitch — it's a genuine reason to connect.

Rules:
- Max 300 characters (LinkedIn limit)
- Reference something specific about their company or work
- No pitch, no services list, no "I'd love to explore synergies"
- Sound like a real person who has a legitimate reason to connect
- One sentence max for why you're reaching out

---
EXAMPLE OUTPUT:

Hi {{contact_name}}, I noticed {{specific_context}} — looks like interesting work. I'm a {{reason_for_fit}} and thought it'd be worth connecting.

---
VARIABLES:
- contact_name: First name
- company_name: Target company
- specific_context: Real, specific observation about their work (max 1 sentence)
- reason_for_fit: Your role/background in one short phrase
- tone: warm_networking
